You Only Get One

ONE BULLET is built around a single, recallable shot. When the bullet leaves your chamber, you’re completely vulnerable. The only way back to safety is skill: movement, timing, and retrieving what you fired.

Enemies & Bosses

Mutated squads with distinct roles. If you hesitate, they flood your angles.

CHARGER Closes distance fast

Forces movement. Punishes tunnel vision. Best answered with a clean recall line.

SHOOTER Suppresses lanes

Turns safe paths into death. Break sightlines or pierce the backline on return.

SHIELDED Frontline wall

Blocks direct shots. Position for the recall angle hit from behind or through gaps.

SPLITTER Multiplies pressure

Ignore it and the arena fills. Kill timing matters: chain it before it becomes a swarm.

Bosses are grotesque, mutated, and unfair.

Each boss warps the arena: toxic zones, collapsing cover, projectile storms, and sudden charges. Your only advantage is perfect spacing and a bullet that obeys your timing.

  • Phase shifts that punish predictable movement
  • Weak points that only open on recall passes
  • Radiation hazards that shrink safe space
